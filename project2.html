Overview

problem

The company's billing process had inaccuracies that were quietly costing revenue, alongside cyber vulnerabilities in the broader digital infrastructure that put the company's data integrity at risk.

approach

Identified the billing errors and led a restructuring of the process around automation. In parallel, identified multiple cybersecurity vulnerabilities and developed SOPs to close them, while managing invoices and totaling services rendered with full accountability and conducting routine system maintenance.

results

The automated billing process increased company revenue and removed the manual errors that had been slipping through, while the new SOPs strengthened the company's digital security posture.
