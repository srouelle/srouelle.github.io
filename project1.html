Overview

problem

Newly acquired customers needed their data migrated onto the platform — but that data was often sensitive, inconsistent, and scattered across multiple source systems, with real risk of errors or exposure during the move.

approach

Cleaned, remapped, and verified variables across databases as part of each onboarding, while troubleshooting errors between management systems via communication APIs. Also built and networked over 25 automated processes with other teams to streamline recurring parts of the workflow, and trained five employees on the SOPs and platforms behind it.

results

Maintained a perfect security track record with zero data breaches across onboardings, and led a data cleaning initiative that resolved hundreds of backlogged support requests — freeing developers to focus on current problems instead of legacy cleanup.
