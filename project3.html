Website Modernization & CRM Buildout

Modernizing a stagnant company website and building a CRM to track customers and target new leads.

Overview

problem

The company's website was stagnant — it couldn't support drop shipping or direct product sales, and the marketing team had no reliable way to track current customers or target new leads.

approach

Modernized the website to enable drop shipping and direct sales, and developed and managed a customer relationship management database for the marketing team. Also handled the day-to-day IT work behind it — system upgrades, user account maintenance, and procurement of new programs.

results

The modernized site drove an increase in company revenue, and the new CRM gave the marketing team a system for tracking customers and targeting new leads for automated business growth.
